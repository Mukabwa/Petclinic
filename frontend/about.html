About Pawtastic

Pawtastic is a modern veterinary clinic founded by Dan Otieno, dedicated to offering the best care for your pets. Our mission is to ensure every pet in Busia is healthy, happy, and loved.

Our Mission

To provide professional veterinary services with love and care, using the latest technology to ensure your pets receive the best possible treatment.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, and excellence in pet care. We treat every animal as if they were our own.

Our Team

Highly trained veterinarians and staff who are passionate about animal health and wellbeing.

Meet Dr. Dan Otieno

With over 10 years of veterinary experience, Dr. Otieno founded Pawtastic to bring modern, compassionate care to Busia's pets. He specializes in preventive care and surgery.

When not at the clinic, Dan enjoys hiking with his rescue dogs, Bella and Max.
